Who Are We?

BlackNdaba Guard (BMGR), operating under Mdu Ntombi Projects (Pty) Ltd, is a registered security services company headquartered in Daveyton, Gauteng. Owned and managed by Mduduzi Ntshangase, MGR provides comprehensive security solutions tailored to the unique needs of residential, commercial, industrial, and government sectors. With PSIRA registration, B-BBEE Level 1 status, and a clear focus on quality, professionalism, and client safety, MGR aims to be a leader in the private security industry.


Our service offerings include physical guarding, alarm response units, mobile patrols, CCTV surveillance, and security consulting. With South Africa's high demand for private security, MGR positions itself as a trusted and cost-effective provider with a competitive edge through our community-based approach, technological integration, and highly trained personnel.



Our financial forecasts indicate steady growth, targeting a 30% annual revenue increase over three years. We aim to secure long-term contracts, enhance brand visibility, and create employment opportunities in local communities. With a robust operational structure, strategic partnerships, and unwavering commitment to quality, MGR is well-positioned to scale sustainably and ethically.
